About Us

"Technical Society of Indian Education - AKGEC (ISTE-AKGEC)" is the national level Society Centre established under Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College . At ISTE , we strive to stimulate interest in techno-managerial stuff among the students of the Instutions . Besides making serious endeavors to spread knowledge on technical stuff we cover all aspects of development through our Multiple domains, we stand together by the stuble acronym of TEAM-'Together Everyone Acheives More'.
